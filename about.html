About our Hotel

Welcome to Kabarak Hotel, where history meets modern luxury in the heart of Nakuru City. Established in 2020, our journey began as a humble inn, evolving into a landmark destination synonymous with exceptional hospitality. With a rich heritage and commitment to excellence, we offer elegantly designed rooms and suites equipped with modern amenities. Enjoy complimentary meals and access to our state-of-the-art gym during your stay. Our passionate team ensures every moment is tailored to exceed expectations, inviting guests to create unforgettable memories. Embrace the essence of hospitality as we continue our legacy of crafting exceptional experiences for each visitor at Kabarak Hotel."
